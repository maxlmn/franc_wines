Théo Blet

Saumur, Loire Valley

Fourth-generation grower who started vinifying just over 1 hectare of his family's best plots. Farmed organically and made with a light touch, these are expressive and energetic wines from shallow clay-limestone soils over tuffeau bedrock. Théo represents the new generation of Loire Valley winemakers, combining traditional knowledge with modern sensibility.

Our Wines

La Peyanne 2022

Chenin Blanc from the estate's finest plots, showcasing the unique terroir of Saumur. Aromas of stone fruits and orange blossom create a wine of exceptional balance and complexity. The shallow clay-limestone soils over tuffeau bedrock contribute to the wine's distinctive minerality and freshness.

Le Corbin 2022

Cabernet Franc offering floral and juicy characteristics with refined structure. This wine exemplifies Théo's light-touch approach to winemaking, allowing the terroir to express itself fully while maintaining the grape's natural elegance and drinkability.

Philosophy & Terroir

Théo Blet's approach to winemaking is rooted in respect for tradition while embracing organic farming principles. The estate's small size allows for meticulous attention to detail, from vineyard management to cellar work. The shallow clay-limestone soils over tuffeau bedrock create wines of exceptional minerality and freshness, while the organic farming practices ensure wines that truly reflect their terroir.
